BILL GATES

Entrepreneur and businessman Bill Gates and his business partner Paul Allen founded and built the world's largest software business, Microsoft, through technological innovation, keen business strategy and aggressive business tactics.

Entrepreneur Bill Gates founded the world's largest software business, Microsoft.. In the process, Gates became one of the richest men in the world.

In February 2014, Gates announced that he was stepping down as Microsoft's chairman to focus on charitable work at his foundation the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation
